# MetaLAWs Cross-Reference Index

*Mapping the 100+ universal laws from the metaLAWs codex to the mythological traditions that embody them.*

---

## L1: Law of Causality
**Every effect has a cause.**
- **Greek-Roman:** Furies enforce consequence chains. The Oresteia transforms retributive causality into institutional.
- **Hindu-Buddhist:** Karma IS this law spiritualized. Autonomous moral causation without external enforcer.
- **Norse:** The Norns weave fate — cause and effect as thread on the loom.
- **Abrahamic:** Covenant theology — do X, receive Y. Contract-as-causality.
- **Regime applications:** All regimes assume this. The Colosseum engine IS causal auditing.

## L3: Law of Entropy
**Systems tend toward disorder without energy input.**
- **Egyptian:** Ma'at vs. Isfet. Article VI of the Constitution derives from this.
- **Hindu-Buddhist:** Yugas — cyclical moral entropy. Satya→Kali decline.
- **Norse:** Ragnarok — entropy wins eventually; governance can only delay.
- **Mesoamerican:** Each Sun destroyed by accumulated structural imbalance.
- **Regime applications:** Egyptian (daily maintenance), Norse (defiant stewardship), Malazan (pressure without release = Cull Song).

## L4: Law of Recursion / Self-Similarity
**Systems mirror themselves at multiple scales.**
- **Abrahamic:** "As above, so below" — Hermetic correspondence. Sefirot map at every scale.
- **Indigenous Australian:** Dreamtime IS this — the creation story repeats at every location along the songline.
- **Zelda:** The triadic cycle repeats in every era — same structure, different actors.
- **Vigiles itself:** The Agon structure mirrors the mythological contests it studies. The system is recursive.

## L5: Law of Contrast / Division
**Meaning is generated by difference.**
- **Daoist:** Yin-yang as the foundational duality. Nothing understood in isolation.
- **WoT:** Saidin/saidar — complementary forces whose separation causes the Taint.
- **Mesoamerican:** Quetzalcoatl/Tezcatlipoca — the eternal adversarial duality.
- **The Agon itself:** Divergence (regimes disagreeing) generates more meaning than consensus.

## L6: Law of Connection / Interdependence
**All things are entangled, materially or symbolically.**
- **Indigenous Australian:** Country, kinship, songlines — everything defined by relationship.
- **African-Yoruba:** 401 Orishas as distributed interdependent governance.
- **One Piece:** Distributed truth — no single source, only the network of sources.
- **Buddhist:** Dependent origination — everything arises from conditions, nothing independent.

## L7: Law of Compensation / Conservation
**For every gain, something is lost or transformed.**
- **Norse:** Odin trades an eye for wisdom. Knowledge costs.
- **Mesoamerican:** Sacrifice as cosmic fuel. Governance consumes resources explicitly.
- **Malazan:** Ascendancy requires trauma/pressure. Power is earned through loss.
- **Egyptian:** The Weighing of the Heart — your life's balance sheet is computed.

## A5: Natural Selection
**Traits that improve survival persist.**
- **Malazan:** Pressure Ascendancy IS natural selection applied to governance.
- **Greek-Roman:** Succession governance — each generation must govern better or be overthrown.
- **JoJo:** Willpower succession — the bloodline that adapts and overcomes across generations.

## B4: Gödel's Incompleteness
**No system can prove all truths about itself.**
- **Daoist:** "The Dao that can be spoken is not the eternal Dao." The system cannot fully know itself.
- **Lynch:** Permeability collapse — reality is not fully knowable from within.
- **The Vigiles:** The Cosmogonists can observe the system but are themselves part of what they observe.

## D6: As Above, So Below
**Patterns repeat across scales.**
- **Abrahamic:** Explicitly stated in Hermetic tradition. Sefirot cascade from Keter to Malkhut.
- **Hindu-Buddhist:** Microcosm/macrocosm — Atman = Brahman.
- **Indigenous Australian:** Every songline location contains the entire Dreaming.
- **Vigiles architecture:** The 8-organ system mirrors the 8-Order Watcher system.

## D7: The Hero's Journey
**Transformation through departure, trial, return.**
- **Greek-Roman:** Odysseus, Heracles, Perseus — the foundational hero cycle.
- **Tolkien:** Frodo's journey — departure from the Shire, trial at Mordor, return transformed.
- **Zelda:** Link's recursive hero cycle — the same journey in every era.
- **One Piece:** Luffy's voyage — departure for the Grand Line, trials at every island, the One Piece as destination.

## E5: Separation of Powers
**Government divided into branches with checks and balances.**
- **Greek-Roman:** Zeus/Poseidon/Hades divide the cosmos by lot. Olympian council consults.
- **Marvel-Cosmic:** Each cosmic entity has single-function jurisdiction.
- **Abrahamic:** Nine choirs in three triads. No level bypasses another.
- **Vigiles:** The 8 Orders ARE separation of powers. Each supreme in its domain, each blind in others'.

## F2: Law of Rhythm
**Everything flows in and out, rises and falls.**
- **Hindu-Buddhist:** Yugas — the cosmic rhythm of ages. Creation→preservation→destruction→renewal.
- **Norse:** Ragnarok as the ultimate rhythmic cycle — destruction followed by rebirth.
- **Egyptian:** The daily solar journey — Ra through the Duat and back. Governance as daily rhythm.
- **WoT:** Ages come and pass. The Wheel turns.
